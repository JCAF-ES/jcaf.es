About me

Whats's up, Dear Reader. I'm Juan Carlos, a junior software developer who believes that if something can be imagined, it can be programmed.I was born in Bolivia and grew up loving art and playing videogames but when I discovered the world of programming, I found my calling.

My first job related to computer engineering was at the Rey Juan Carlos University (Fuenlabrada). At the URJC I consolidated the concepts I had of the network structure and I learned to support users. In my second job at the DGT, I applied everything I learned at the URJC and thanks to this I was able to acquire a lot of responsibility. In my last job at Grupo Primavera, I learned to use many of the Microsoft tools, so I was able to acquire knowledge to prepare for the Microsoft AZ900 exam, which I hope to take soon.

This is a summary of the work I have done so far. My goal is to keep learning because software development is what I'm passionate about.
